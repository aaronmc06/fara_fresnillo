SAFETY OBSERVATION

Observe employee(s) for several minutes. Note the type of work they are performing. Check the boxes that apply. Rate each checked box as to 'safe' or 'at risk' behaviours. Stop or correct any unsafe acts or conditions that could cause injury or harm to the employee or the equipment.
